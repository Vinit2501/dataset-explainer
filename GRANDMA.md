# My Project: The Spreadsheet Helper

Imagine you are handed a giant, messy spreadsheet full of numbers and words, and you have no idea what any of it means. 

I built a website where you can paste that mess in. My program quickly reads the text, tells you exactly what kind of information you are looking at (like ages, job titles, or money), and suggests the best mathematical way to use that data to predict things in the future. 

I built it because people who work with data waste hours just trying to figure out the basics of a new file. My tool does that boring part for them instantly.
